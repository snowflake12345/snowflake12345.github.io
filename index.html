Poems

What are they:

Poems are pieces of text written to tell something or to express someone's feelings. They are written in seperate lines that often have a repeated rhythm or sometimes rhyme. It is easy to write a poem by yourself.

Different types of poems:

  • Limerick
  • Acrostic
  • Haiku
  • Sonnet
  • Lyric

How to write different poems:

Limerick Structure:

It has to have 5 lines

First Line: 7-10 syllables
Second Line: 7-10 syllables
Third Line: 5-7 syllables
Fourth Line: 5-7 syllables
Fifth Line: 7-10 syllables

Acrostic Structure:

It has to be made on a Base word

You need to pick a word
Write it down vertically
Write down a phrase or message for each letter

Haiku Structure

It consists of 3 lines:

First Line:5 syllables
Second Line: 7 syllables
Third Line: 5 syllables
